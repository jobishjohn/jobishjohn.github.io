I am an Assistant Professor (Adhoc) in the Computer Science and Engineering department at Indian Intitute of Information Technology (IIIT) Kottayam. My research interests are in the area of Embedded Systems and Communication Networks; especially in Wireless Sensor Networks (WSN), Low -Power Wide Area Networks (LP-WAN), and Internet of Things (IoT).

I received B.Tech in Electronics and Communication from College of Engineerig Chengannur in 2007 and Ph.D in Electrical Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay in 2020.
